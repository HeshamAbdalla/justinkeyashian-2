Justin Keyashian, DDS

Who I Am

Hi there, I am Justin Keyashian. I graduated from NYU College of Dentistry in 2010 I believes everyone should have access to affordable, high quality dentistry. I worked diligently on perfecting my skills so that I can deliver the highest quality dentistry that is both fast and painless.

I am more than proficient at doing all procedures from root canals and extractions to bridges and dentures. I also really enjoy working with kids. Their energy and innocence make them a little unpredictable and a lot of fun.

Before NYU

I grew up in Chicago where I was always interested in science and health. This led me to UCLA where I studied Physiological Sciences. Initially working the front desk at a dental office during my undergrad, I quickly realized my passion for dentistry.

The profession is the perfect mix of science, health, art and small business. It was at this time that I decided that I wanted to become a great general dentist that has mastered all aspects of the dental field. I didn't want to just place filling and crowns, I wanted to be a head and neck physician. This idea is what led me to NYU.

Overall full body health being very important to me, I like to exercise in my free time. I also like to watch movies and hang out with friends. Family is very important to me, so I spend the occasional weekend visiting my family in Walnut Creek where I also enjoy spending time working on my car.
